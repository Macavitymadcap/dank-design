// Basic responsive image

Object-fit Utilities

Object-fit: Cover

.object-cover - Fills the container while maintaining aspect ratio, may crop the image

Object-fit: Contain

.object-contain - Ensures the entire image is visible, may leave empty space

Object-fit: Fill

.object-fill - Stretches the image to fill the container regardless of aspect ratio
